Frequently Asked Questions
How do I choose the right business idea?⌄
Start by evaluating your skills, available capital, and time commitment. Filter ideas by difficulty level and investment required. Our platform shows profit potential and time-to-launch for each idea to help you make an informed decision.
Do I need technical skills to start most of these businesses?⌄
Not at all! Many ideas like Digital Marketing, E-Commerce (Dropshipping), and Content Writing require zero technical skills. Each card clearly shows the difficulty level so you can find the perfect match for your background.
What's the minimum investment needed to start?⌄
Some ideas like Freelancing and Content Writing can be started with $0. Others like SaaS Development may require $500-$5,000 for tools, hosting, and marketing. We display investment ranges clearly on every card.
How long does it take to see results?⌄
Freelancing and Digital Marketing can generate income within 2-4 weeks. SaaS and App Development typically take 3-6 months. E-Commerce businesses usually break even within 1-3 months with the right strategy.
Is the platform free to use?⌄
Yes! Basic access is completely free. Premium membership unlocks advanced analytics, personalized AI recommendations, community access, and detailed implementation roadmaps for each business idea.
